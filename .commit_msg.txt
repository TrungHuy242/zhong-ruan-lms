Refactor: Tái cấu trúc Frontend theo Feature-Based Architecture

- app/: layout, providers, guards, routes - các thành phần xuyên suốt app
- shared/: chia theo domain (api, storage, contexts, validation, components/ui)
- features/<x>/: mỗi feature có pages/, components/, services/, types/, constants/, index.ts
  + services/<feature>Api.ts: tầng giao tiếp backend
  + types/<feature>.types.ts: type/interface
  + constants/<feature>.constants.ts: const + validation
  + index.ts: barrel export

Di chuyển:
- Header/Sidebar/Footer/AdminLayout từ shared/components/layout/ -> app/layouts/
- ProtectedRoute từ shared/components/guards/ -> app/guards/
- NotificationProvider bọc toàn cục trong app/providers/AppProviders (mount từ main.tsx)
- App.tsx chỉ render <AppRoutes />; toàn bộ routing chuyển sang app/routes/AppRoutes.tsx

Tách types/constants:
- users, notifications, files, audit-log, settings, profile, search, trash đều có types/ + constants/ riêng
- Một số module (auth, dashboard) chỉ cần services vì không có types/constants riêng
